Profile

Max Emilian Verstappen (born September 30, 1997) is a professional racing driver from the Netherlands who currently competes in Formula One with the Red Bull Racing team. He's one of the most talented young drivers in F1 history and is known for his aggressive racing style and his amazing ability in wet track conditions.

Biodata

Verstappen was born in Hasselt, Belgium. Racing blood runs deep in his veins, since his father, Jos Verstappen, was also a former Formula 1 driver, while his mother, Sophie Kumpen, was a successful former karting racer.
